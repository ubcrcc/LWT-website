Past Events

First Aid Jeopardy February 28th, 2025

Join us in ANGU 293 at 6-7PM for a night of first aid and Red Cross themed Jeopardy for a chance to win a gift card! Admission is free and snacks and drinks are provided.

Overdose Prevention Society Safety Packaging February 26th, 2025

Come out to BUCH D205 from 6-7PM and help us assemble overdose prevention packages. Attendees will help package safety injection kits and learn about harm reduction sites, admission is free!

General Meeting February 10th, 2025

Come to our first general meeting of the term, where you can see what our committees have been up to and what our plans are for the next few months. In addition, we’ll be hosting naloxone training!

Dough-Nate for a Cause October 2nd - 4th, 2024

This fundraiser, in collaboration with Sweet Success, is an exciting event where attendees can enjoy delicious, high-protein donuts while supporting a great cause. This unique twist on a classic treat combines indulgence with health-conscious choices, perfect for students and fitness enthusiasts alike.

Naloxone Training October 30th, 2024

This free event facilitated by the AMS Peer Support is a great way to learn how and when to use a naloxone kit!

Loving Spoonful October 19th, 2025

A Loving Spoonful is a volunteer-run not-for-profit organization that provides free meals for individuals and families living with HIV and co-existing illnesses in the Downtown Eastside. Together, the Outreach & Awareness committee at UBC Red Cross is offering an off-campus volunteer and awareness opportunity for UBC students to take part in assembling, packing and labeling meals at their Downtown Eastside location.
